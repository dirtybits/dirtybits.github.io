ABOUT CONCEAL

Our network is completely decentralised, without the need for a central server or trusted parties. Users hold the crypto keys to their own money and transact directly with each other over a secure P2P network. On top of it, Conceal has a decentralised banking system and instant encrypted messages on its core..

There is a locked Foundation Trust of 12mm CCX set aside for future acquisitions, partnerships, and emergencies. In addition, there is a campaign fund of 3mm CCX (exchanges, marketing, and events), a development fund of 666,666 CCX (research, development, operational expenses, team, and collaborators), and a community fund of 333,333 CCX (airdrops, beta-testers, bounties, prizes, and activities), which unlock at blocks 65700, 25063, and 24320 respectively.
